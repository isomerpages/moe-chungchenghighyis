<p><strong>Subject: Congratulations, Graduands! Details for the Release of 2025 GCE ‘O’ Level Examination Results</strong>
</p>
<p>Dear Graduands,</p>
<p>We are excited for you as you reach this significant milestone! We warmly
invite you back to school for the release of your 2025 GCE ‘O’ Level Examination
Results.</p>
<p>Please find the important details for the event below:</p>
<p><strong>Date</strong>: Wednesday, 14 January 2026</p>
<p><strong>Time</strong>: 2.00 p.m. (Please be seated by <strong>1.45 p.m.</strong>)</p>
<p><strong>Venue</strong>: Multi-Purpose Hall (MPH)</p>
<p><strong>Attire</strong>
</p>
<p>To respect the formality of this important occasion, we require all graduands
to be in Full School Uniform.</p>
<p>Please ensure your hair is neat and complies fully with all school rules
(i.e., no coloured or long hair).</p>
<p>Graduands who are unable to meet these attire requirements will be directed
to an alternate designated area to collect their result slip and certificate
from 3.30 p.m. onwards.</p>
<p><strong>Guests</strong>
</p>
<p>We encourage you to share this moment! Each graduand may invite a maximum
of two (2) guests to attend the ceremony with you.</p>
<p><strong>Proxy Collection (If Unable to Attend)</strong>
</p>
<p>If you are unable to attend the ceremony, please contact your Form Teacher
immediately to obtain and complete a proxy form.</p>
<p>• The completed proxy form must be submitted for your proxy to collect
the result slip and certificate.</p>
<p>• Only close relatives are permitted to act as your authorized proxy.</p>
<p>We are immensely proud of your hard work and achievements. We wish you
the very best for your results and your exciting future ahead!</p>
